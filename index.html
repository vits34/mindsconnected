Impactful Stories

Real situations. Real decisions. Real outcomes.

Story 01

Sprint Cancellation That Transformed Team Ownership

Challenge

Product Owner and stakeholder identified current Sprint work as irrelevant to users and product value.

Action

Boldly supported the decision to cancel the Sprint, prioritising product value over adherence to outdated plans. Facilitated an immediate Sprint Planning focused on valuable outcomes.

Impact

Prevented 2 weeks of wasted effort and triggered an exponential increase in team ownership and involvement in product decisions.

Story 02

Executive–Product Owner Alignment

Challenge

Executive team and Product Owner community struggled with priority alignment and understanding product synergies.

Action

Collaborated with the PO community to generate alignment solutions, unifying both groups through strategic in-person sessions focused on honest communication.

Impact

Alignment sessions became a mandatory quarterly practice with a structured approach to collaborative decision-making between C-level and product teams.

Story 03

OKR Implementation at Scale

Challenge

Teams lacked alignment on quarterly and yearly objectives, with leadership seeking better methods to unite everyone toward a shared purpose.

Action

Collaborated with leadership to design a year-round OKR system, guiding collective outcome achievement across the entire organisation.

Impact

Over two years, the organisation transformed its structure and value streams — from small first-year wins to exceptional second-year alignment and true shared goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we get started.

How long does a typical engagement take? +

Engagements range from a focused 4-week sprint to multi-year transformation partnerships. We always start with a discovery session to scope the right approach for your situation.

Do you work remotely or on-site? +

Both. We are fully remote-capable and have worked with clients across multiple continents. We also love getting on-site when the situation calls for it — real collaboration happens everywhere.

Do you work with companies of all sizes? +

Yes. We work with startups finding product-market fit, mid-sized companies scaling their teams, and large enterprises navigating complex transformations. The approach adapts — the commitment doesn't.

How do we get started? +

Simply reach out via our contact form or email. We'll schedule a no-obligation discovery call to understand your needs and see if we're the right fit for each other.

What makes Minds Connected different? +

We don't deliver a report and walk away. We work alongside your teams, transfer knowledge, and make sure the changes stick long after the engagement ends. Co-creating is in our DNA.
